Our Services

Comprehensive care for your home

Professional house sitting designed to protect and maintain your property while you're away.

Property monitoring & security

Daily walkthroughs of your entire property, checking all entry points, windows, and security systems. We collect mail and packages, adjust lighting, and maintain a lived-in appearance to deter potential intruders.

Maintenance care & checks

Regular inspection of HVAC systems, plumbing, and appliances. We water plants, maintain pool chemistry, run faucets to prevent pipe issues, and ensure all systems function properly.

Emergency response

24/7 availability to handle unexpected situations. We coordinate with contractors, manage repairs, and make critical decisions to protect your property. Our rapid response minimizes damage.
