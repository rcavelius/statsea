FAQ

Questions we hear most

Are you bonded and insured?+

Yes. We carry full liability insurance and are bonded for your protection. We're happy to provide proof of coverage before we start.

How do you access my home?+

We work with whatever method you prefer — spare key, lockbox, smart lock code, or garage code. All access information is kept strictly confidential.

What happens if something goes wrong?+

We respond immediately. Whether it's a burst pipe, a power outage, or an alarm trigger, we coordinate with contractors and emergency services in real time.

How often will you visit?+

That's up to you. Most clients choose daily visits, but we offer everything from weekly check-ins to twice-daily walkthroughs.

What areas do you serve?+

Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Highland Beach, Deerfield Beach, and Boynton Beach.

Traveling soon?

Let's set up your care plan before you leave. Free consultation, no commitment.
